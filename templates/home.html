Welcome to Blood-Bank Monitoring System

Most people are able to donate blood, but only 4% actually do it. You can donate blood if you:

  • Are fit and healthy
  • Are not suffering from transmittable diseases
  • Are 18-65 years old and Weigh at least 45kg
  • Have Haemoglobin not less than 12.5 grams
